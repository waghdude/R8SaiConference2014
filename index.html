Region 8 Sai Conference Registration

Sai Ram! The Annual Sathya Sai Baba Conference for Region 8 will be held from Saturday, August 30, 2014 to Sunday, August 31, 2014. This year's conference theme is “Living in His Omnipresence.”

The conference will be held at the Duarte Performing Arts Center, 1401 Highland Avenue, Duarte, California 91010.

The registration fees are $10 per day for each adult (age 19 and over). Children and youth 18 years and younger are free.

Parents of children aged 6-18 are urged to register them for Sai Spiritual Education (SSE) for both days. The SSE program will focus on spiritual, fun-filled sessions in the morning and afternoon, and will lead to a presentation on Sunday afternoon.

REGISTRATION DEADLINE: SATURDAY, AUGUST 16, 2014

Please register before the deadline in order to help organizers better plan means and other arrangements for the event.
